Space Race!

    How to Play

  • There are three speeds your ship can be set to:
    • Fast - 2m/Turn
    • Slow - 1m/Turn
    • Coast - 0m/Turn
  • You can set the rotation of your ship to any degrees between 0(starting eastward!) to 360(also eastward!)
  • Now remember your ship will continue to move at what ever speed you set
  • So if you're moving too fast you will fly out of the assigned testing area!
